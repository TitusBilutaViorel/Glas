Brand Goals

Our initiative is to create
a streetwear brand.

By establishing such a brand, we would like to value principles such as individuality, uniqueness and
self-expression of buyers.

We want this initiative to represent a means of identifying people with their own values and preferences of an aesthetic and artistic nature, as our brand serves as an original alternative of local producers to fast fashion invaded by generic western influences and acts of plagiarism of some other well-known fashion houses / brands.
